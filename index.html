About Me

My name is Nataly Garusova. I am Russian but lived in Uzbekistan all of my life. We moved to Colorado seven years ago, and I enjoy living here!

My passion for coding and web development started after visiting different websites and applications. It was always interesting to me how sites were built. Therefore, I decided to gain formal education through attending Web Development and SQL at Arapahoe Community College. After pursuing these classes, I decided that my aspirations were for development and decided to take additional courses at the Coding Boot Camp at the University of Denver. I absolutely love coding and enjoy building something from scratch!
